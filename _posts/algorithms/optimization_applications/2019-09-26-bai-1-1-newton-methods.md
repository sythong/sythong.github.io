# Optimization
## Optimization problem:
 A generic optimization problem 
        minimize $f(x)$
        subject to $x \in X$ 
- $x \in R^n $ is the optimization variable

## Probems:

- Taylor: 
$$ q(x) = f(x^k) + f'(x^k) (x-x^k) $$
